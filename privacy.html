1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the App. Here is a breakdown of what we collect:

Account Information

Email address Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
Name If provided during sign-up or via social login (Google, Apple)
Authentication data Encrypted passwords or OAuth tokens (Google Sign-In, Sign in with Apple)

Financial Data You Provide

Portfolio data Portfolio names, types, and settings you create
Transaction data Stock, crypto, options, and other transactions you manually enter or import
Holdings Calculated from your transactions — shares, cost basis, and current values

Brokerage Data (via Plaid)

Account info Brokerage account names and identifiers retrieved through Plaid

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: precise location data, contacts, photos, browsing history, advertising identifiers, or any data from other apps on your device. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not serve ads.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through three methods:

  • Directly from you — when you create an account, manually enter transactions, create portfolios, submit feedback, or configure settings
  • From third-party services you connect — when you link a brokerage account through Plaid, we receive authorized account and transaction data
  • Automatically from market data providers — we fetch publicly available stock and cryptocurrency prices from third-party data sources to display current values in the App. This data is not personal information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide the App's core functionality — displaying your portfolios, calculating holdings, showing performance data, and syncing across devices
  • Authenticate your account — verifying your identity when you sign in
  • Sync your data — keeping your portfolios and transactions consistent across multiple devices via our cloud service
  • Import brokerage data — processing transactions from linked brokerage accounts through Plaid
  • Display market data — fetching current prices and financial data for the securities in your portfolios
  • Respond to your feedback — if you submit feedback through the App, we use your contact information and device details to respond and address issues
  • Improve the App — understanding usage patterns to fix bugs and develop new features
  • Communicate with you — sending account-related notifications such as subscription changes or Terms updates
We do not: sell your data, use your data for advertising, create advertising profiles, share your financial data with data brokers, or use your data for any purpose unrelated to providing and improving the App.

4. Data Storage & Security

On-Device Storage

Your portfolio and transaction data is stored locally on your device using the iOS Keychain, which provides hardware-level encryption. This data is encrypted at rest, accessible only when your device is unlocked, and is not included in device backups. App preferences and settings are stored separately using standard iOS storage.

Cloud Storage

If you create an account, your data is also synced to our cloud database hosted on Supabase, a secure cloud infrastructure provider. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS (HTTPS) and at rest on the server. Access to the database is restricted to authenticated users and limited to their own data through row-level security policies.

Authentication Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols. We never store your third-party account passwords.

No system is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and account credentials.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — with the third-party services listed above, solely to the extent necessary to operate the App
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Protection of rights — to enforce our Terms of Service, protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users or the public
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, in which case your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data from our servers, including your portfolios, transactions, holdings, and profile information.

Some data may be retained for a limited period after deletion for the following reasons:

  • To comply with legal obligations (such as tax or financial recordkeeping requirements)
  • To resolve disputes or enforce our agreements
  • In anonymized or aggregated form that cannot identify you, for analytical purposes

Data stored locally on your device via the iOS Keychain is removed when you delete the App from your device.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — you can view all of your data directly within the App at any time
  • Correction — you can edit your transactions, portfolios, and account information through the App
  • Deletion — you can delete your account at any time through the App's Settings. This will remove your data from our servers
  • Data export — your transaction data is visible in the App and can be used as your personal record
  • Unlink brokerage accounts — you can disconnect linked brokerage accounts at any time through the App's Settings, which revokes Plaid's access to your brokerage data
  • Withdraw consent — you can stop using the App at any time. Deleting the App removes local data; deleting your account removes cloud data

To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions, contact us at bigyesh@shatisand.com.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with additional rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know — you may request that we disclose what personal information we have collected, used, disclosed, and sold about you in the past 12 months
  • Right to delete — you may request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to opt out of sale — we do not sell your personal information and have not done so in the preceding 12 months
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at bigyesh@shatisand.com. We will respond to verifiable consumer requests within 45 days.
